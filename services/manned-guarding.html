Our process

From enquiry to deployment in days, not weeks

Site survey

A senior member of our team visits, listens, and produces a written risk assessment — free and without obligation.

Solution design

We design assignment instructions, shift patterns, escalation routes and KPIs around the risks identified.

Officer selection

Officers are selected for the site — vetted to BS 7858, briefed and inducted before the first shift.

Live operations

Real-time reporting, supervisor checks, and monthly performance reviews keep the contract honest and improving.

Frequently asked questions

Are all your officers SIA-licensed?
Yes. Every officer we deploy holds a valid SIA licence appropriate to their role, and is vetted to BS 7858 standards as part of our SIA Approved Contractor commitments.
How quickly can you mobilise a new contract?
For most assignments we can have a fully briefed officer on site within 48–72 hours of agreement. Emergency cover is often possible the same day.
Do you cover holidays and sickness?
Yes — our schedules are designed with built-in resilience, and our supervisor pool covers absence so you never have an unmanned post.
Do I get reporting and visibility?
Every officer logs activity through a digital reporting platform with scanned patrol points. You'll receive weekly summary reports and a monthly contract review.
