Sectors

Specialists in your environment

Nine sectors, one accountable partner. Our officers, cleaners and facilities teams are trained for the rhythm of your operation — and we measure success against the standards that matter in your industry.

Education

So teaching staff can focus on teaching

Primary schools, sixth-form colleges and university campuses — we deliver security, grounds, cleaning and reactive maintenance under one budget-aware contract. Staff are vetted to enhanced DBS standards for educational suitability, and we provide emergency cover for plumbing, HVAC and electrical failures so a broken boiler doesn't close a school day.

Medical

Hospital-grade hygiene, healthcare-grade discretion

Clinics, surgeries, hospital wings and pharmaceutical sites need cleaning protocols that satisfy infection control, plus security that protects patient data, drugs and equipment around the clock. Our teams work to hospital-grade disinfection standards in patient rooms and theatres, and our officers train on confidentiality before they're cleared to attend.

Manufacturing

Production stops cost more than security does

Factories live and die by uptime. We deliver proactive maintenance, industrial cleaning and 24/7 surveillance designed to protect machinery, materials and intellectual property — without disrupting shift patterns. Our officers and engineers coordinate around your production calendar, so cover is in place before a line stops, not after.

Construction

Site protection that pays for itself

Plant theft, contractor sign-in chaos and vandalism slow projects more than weather does. Our manned guarding, mobile patrols and deployable CCTV protect plant, materials and access points around the clock. Safety officers handle contractor inductions, fire-route checks and waste-area enforcement — so the site stays compliant and on schedule.

Corporate Office

First impressions count — your front desk is one

Outsourced reception, concierge, cleaning and security for corporate HQs and multi-tenant buildings. Uniformed front-of-house officers manage visitors and access control; cleaning teams keep client-facing areas to a hospitality standard; and CCTV, alarm and concierge software run on shared infrastructure. One supplier, one bill, one account manager.

Retail

Less shrinkage, calmer stores, sharper presentation

Supermarkets, banks, shopping centres and high-street stores all face the same equation — keep loss down, keep visitors welcome. SIA-licensed officers (ACT and SCaN-trained) handle store cover and behaviour profiling; cleaning teams keep concourses and shopfronts presentable through trading hours; alarms and CCTV are professionally monitored from our control room.

Retail security →
Transport

People keep moving. Your facilities have to keep up

Stations, depots, terminals and ports — high-footfall environments where security failures are public and disruption is expensive. We deliver access control, threat-detection-trained officers and 24/7 ARC monitoring alongside the cleaning, signage and maintenance work that keeps the experience consistent for passengers and operators alike.

Live monitoring →
Leisure

Open or closed, your venue is always representing

Hotels, gyms, members' clubs, stadia and event venues need security and facilities cover that flexes with the calendar. We handle quiet-period maintenance and patrol, scale up for event days with SIA stewards and crowd-safe officers, then turn the venue around overnight. Sustainability and waste reduction are built into every contract.

Warehouses

The supply chain depends on a tight perimeter

Distribution centres, fulfilment hubs and bonded warehouses run on hours and pallets, not days. We supply HGV gatehouse cover, ANPR-linked access control, perimeter CCTV and on-site industrial cleaning — scaled up for peak season, down for quiet weeks. The cost line moves with your throughput, not against it.
