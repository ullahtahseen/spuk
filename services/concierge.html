Concierge & Reception

Concierge officers who set the tone the moment guests arrive

Polished, articulate, and SIA-trained — our concierge team blend genuine hospitality with quiet vigilance, protecting your premises without ever feeling like security.

What we deliver

The first impression and the last line of defence

Our concierge officers are recruited as much for their bearing and language as their licence. They handle visitor management, deliveries, lost property and resident requests — while quietly monitoring access, CCTV and emergency systems behind the scenes.

  • SIA-licensed, customer-service trained
  • Site-specific induction including first aid & fire
  • Visitor and contractor management systems
  • Concierge software, parcel logs, key control
  • Brand-aligned uniform, presentation reviewed weekly
