High-Level Access & Cleaning

Windows clean enough to disappear

From shopfronts to tower glazing — pole-fed, cradle-access and abseil window cleaning, fully insured, IPAF and IRATA-trained where it matters.

What we deliver

Right method. Right access. Right paperwork.

Most window-cleaning problems come from choosing the wrong access method or skipping the paperwork. We carry out a written risk assessment and method statement (RAMS) for every site, and match the access technique to the building rather than the contractor.

  • Pure-water pole systems for low- and mid-rise
  • IPAF-certified MEWP operators for elevated work
  • IRATA-trained abseil teams for high-rise
  • Site-specific RAMS and £10m public liability
  • Frame, cill and cladding cleaning included
