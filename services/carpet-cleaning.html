Carpets that look five years younger by Monday morning

Hot-water extraction, low-moisture encapsulation and stain treatment for office, hotel and retail carpets — scheduled around your trading hours.

What we deliver

The right method for the carpet you actually have

Wool carpets, nylon contract carpets and luxury vinyl tiles all need different treatments — and the wrong choice shrinks fibres or strips the finish. Our technicians inspect every carpet before they price, and choose the method to suit the fibre, the soil and the trading window you have available.

  • Hot-water extraction for deep clean
  • Encapsulation for low-moisture and faster drying
  • Spot, stain and odour treatment
  • Upholstery and soft-furnishing care
  • Out-of-hours and weekend scheduling
