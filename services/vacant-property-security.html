Empty Buildings

Vacant doesn't have to mean vulnerable

Insurance-compliant inspections, steel boarding, on-site presence and rapid response — protecting empty buildings from squatters, vandalism, theft and water damage.

What we deliver

Layered protection from day one of the void

Empty property is at risk the moment the last occupant leaves — and most insurance policies require documented inspections at least weekly. We layer physical measures (steel screens, boarding, manned cover) with electronic (CCTV, alarms, sensors) and provide the paperwork your insurer asks for.

  • Weekly or daily documented inspections
  • Steel screens, boarding and lock changes
  • On-site SIA-licensed cover where required
  • Deployable CCTV towers and alarm systems
  • Squatter removal liaison & legal documentation
