Grounds & Landscaping

Grounds that say something the moment you arrive

Year-round lawn care, hedge trimming, weed control and seasonal planting for corporate, residential and industrial estates — keeping kerb appeal sharp through every season.

What we deliver

A schedule that survives British weather

Grounds work isn't only about looking good — it's about safe footways, clear sight-lines and well-lit boundaries. Our teams are trained in health-and-safety as well as horticulture, and we coordinate visits with your security cover so contractors aren't on site unsupervised.

  • Scheduled visits, year-round, weather-resilient
  • Lawn care, hedges, weed control and planting
  • Tree surveys and conservation-area awareness
  • Sight-line and boundary management for security
  • Winter gritting and snow clearance available

Scope of works

What a standard programme covers

Lawn care

Mowing, edging, scarification, aeration and feeding — kept on a schedule that matches growth and weather.

Hedges & shrubs

Trimming, shaping and seasonal pruning — keeping boundaries crisp and sight-lines clear for CCTV.

Weed & moss control

Hard-standing, kerb-line and gravel areas treated to keep entrances and car parks looking right.

Seasonal planting

Spring and summer planting plans, autumn bedding refresh, winter structural planting for year-round colour.

Tree management

Tree surveys, crown lifts and dead-wood removal — handled with arboricultural assessments where needed.

Winter response

Snow clearance, salt and grit application, ice-watch overnight cover for sites that can't risk a closure.
