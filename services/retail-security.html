Retail & Loss Prevention

Protect your stock, your staff, and your customer experience

Retail-specialist officers trained beyond the SIA minimum — using behaviour profiling, investigation skills and disciplined reporting to drive measurable reductions in shrinkage.

Training that goes further

Retail crime needs more than a uniform — it needs craft

Our retail officers complete additional training in ACT (Action Counters Terrorism), SCaN (See, Check and Notify) and behavioural profiling — both elements that are missing from the mandatory SIA syllabus. The result is a guard who can spot patterns, anticipate offenders, and prevent loss before it happens.

  • ACT, SCaN and behavioural profiling certification
  • Repeat offender identification and intel sharing
  • Detailed, court-ready incident report writing
  • Customer-facing demeanour — never aggressive
  • Plain-clothes and uniformed deployments
