Washroom Hygiene

Washrooms that hold up to a visitor's first glance

Daily deep cleans, scheduled sanitary disposal and consumable replenishment — keeping every washroom on your estate compliant, stocked and presentable.

What we deliver

Hygiene the way an environmental-health inspector reads it

Washrooms are the single fastest way to lose trust with a visitor — and they're inspected line-by-line by environmental health. Our programme covers deep cleaning, scheduled sanitary disposal, consumable replenishment and odour control, all on a written specification with signed visit cards.

  • Deep clean of pans, urinals, basins and surfaces
  • Consumables: soap, paper, sanitiser, fragrance
  • Compliant feminine hygiene disposal
  • Air-care, odour control and fly units
  • Signed visit cards on the back of every door
