Privacy Policy

How Security Projects UK Limited collects, uses and protects personal data.

Last updated: January 2026

Who we are

Security Projects UK Limited (company number 6808071) is the data controller for personal information collected through this website. Our registered office is Estate House, Marsh Way, Fairview Industrial Estate, Rainham, Essex, RM13 8UH.

What we collect

When you contact us, we collect the information you provide — typically your name, email address, telephone number, company, location and the details of your enquiry. We do not collect special-category personal data through this website.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to your enquiry, prepare a quotation, and where you become a client, to deliver and administer your contract. We may keep your enquiry on file for up to 24 months for follow-up. We will never sell your information.

Lawful basis

We rely on legitimate interests (responding to your enquiry) and, where you become a client, contract performance. Where required, we obtain your consent — for example for marketing emails, which you can withdraw at any time.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies without your consent. See our cookies page for details.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict our use of your personal data, and to object to processing. To exercise any of these rights please contact us at info@securityprojectsltd.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

Data security

We hold your information on secure, access-controlled systems. We retain it only for as long as necessary and dispose of it securely.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at info@securityprojectsltd.co.uk or by post at the address above.
