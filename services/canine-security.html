Dog Handler Teams

Visible deterrence. Unmatched detection.

NASDU-trained handlers paired with licensed security dogs — patrol, search and detection across large sites, events, construction and high-risk premises.

What we deliver

One handler-dog team often replaces three officers

A trained dog covers ground faster than a foot patrol, detects intrusion through scent and sound that humans miss, and provides a level of visible deterrence that few other security measures match. Our handlers are SIA-licensed and NASDU-trained, working with dogs from licensed providers under strict welfare standards.

  • NASDU Level 2 trained handlers
  • SIA Door Supervisor licensed where required
  • General Purpose, search and detection dogs
  • Full welfare documentation and vet records
  • One-off, event and contract deployments
