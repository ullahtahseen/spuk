About us

A security company built on accountability, not slogans

Security Projects UK Limited has protected businesses across London, Essex and the Home Counties for over a decade — with directors who came up through the front line and stay close to every contract.

Our story

Founded in 2009. Still answering the phones ourselves.

Security Projects UK Limited was founded by Mark White in 2009 to do something the industry had stopped doing well — treat security as a craft and a partnership, not a commodity. Mark came from twenty years on the front line, including covert investigations, work alongside the Metropolitan Police, and protection assignments for blue-chip and high-net-worth clients.

More than fifteen years on, our directors still walk client sites, still know every supervisor by name, and still pick up the phone when something goes wrong. Our clients stay with us for ten, fifteen, twenty years — that's the only review that matters.

We hold SIA Approved Contractor Scheme (ACS) status, and our management team brings over 150 years of combined experience across security, policing, hospitality, retail and high-risk environments.

What we believe

Three principles, applied to every contract

Be the people you call

Senior leaders are accessible — not behind a contact centre. The same person who designs your contract will pick up the phone at 3am if there's an incident.

Train beyond the minimum

SIA licensing is the floor, not the ceiling. We invest in ACT, SCaN and behavioural training so our officers see what others miss.

Measure what matters

Real-time reporting, supervisor checks and monthly reviews mean you always know how your contract is performing — and so do we.
