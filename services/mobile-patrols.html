Mobile Patrols

Eyes on your site, even when no one's there

GPS-tracked patrol vehicles, real-time monitored from our 24/7 control room — the most cost-effective way to maintain a security presence across single or multiple sites.

How it works

Live-monitored mobile presence, not just a drive-by

Every patrol vehicle is tracked in real time with live video transmission to our 24/7 control room. Officers scan RFID checkpoints at agreed locations and log activity digitally, so you always know — to the minute — when and where they attended.

  • GPS vehicle tracking and route verification
  • Live video feed monitored from control room
  • RFID checkpoint scanning at every visit
  • Digital, time-stamped patrol reports
  • Officers focused on security — nothing else

Patrol options

From single visits to multi-mile coverage

Scheduled patrols

Fixed-time visits at intervals you specify — ideal for known-risk windows like overnight or weekends.

Randomised patrols

Visits that vary deliberately to prevent pattern-spotting — preferred by insurers for high-value sites.

Lock-up & unlock

Opening and closing of premises with checks, alarm setting, and confirmation reports.

BID & estate coverage

Multi-site routes including BIDs — we currently cover the 7-mile London Riverside BID.

Alarm response

Rapid response to confirmed activations — investigate, secure, and report back within agreed SLAs.

Welfare & void checks

Empty property inspections for landlords, agents and developers — interior and exterior reports.
