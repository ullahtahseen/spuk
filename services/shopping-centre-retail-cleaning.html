Retail Environments

Cleaning that keeps the centre trading

Public concourses, escalators, lift lobbies, food courts and washrooms — kept spotless through trading hours without ever blocking footfall.

What we deliver

Customer experience starts at the floor

Shoppers form their impression of a centre in the first ten seconds. Our retail cleaning teams work the public areas while trade is live — discreet, uniformed, and trained not to obstruct the visitor journey. Out-of-hours we handle deep cleans, floor refinishing, escalator handrail sanitising and shopfront glazing.

  • Daytime concourse cleaning, low-profile
  • Escalator and travellator handrail sanitising
  • Public washroom & baby-change attendant cover
  • Floor refinish, scrubber-drier and burnishing
  • End-of-lease and unit handover cleans
