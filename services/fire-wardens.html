Fire Safety Personnel

Trained fire wardens and marshals — on contract or on call

Fire-safety personnel for offices, events, construction and high-occupancy residential buildings. NEBOSH-aware, evacuation-trained, and ready to step in when your own warden is absent.

What we deliver

Cover that meets your Fire Risk Assessment

Fire Risk Assessments specify warden numbers and competencies — but holiday, sickness and turnover often leave gaps. We supply trained fire wardens and marshals to fill rotas, cover events, or staff up during high-occupancy periods.

  • Trained in fire safety, evacuation and roll call
  • Daily building inspections and log-book keeping
  • Event and high-occupancy cover
  • Disabled-persons evac (PEEP) trained where required
  • Same-day cover available
